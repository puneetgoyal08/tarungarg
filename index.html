SURGICAL TREATMENTS

Specializing in minimally invasive brain & spine surgery, Dr. Tarun Garg leaves no stone unturned when it comes to providing state-of-the-art treatment as a neurosurgeon. He is one among the few brain & spine surgeons in North India proficient in complex procedures like brain tumors, haemorrhages, spinal deformity/tumor treatments, spasticity procedures, and many more, as you can see below. Expertise in these techniques has placed Dr. Tarun firmly atop the ranks of the best neurosurgeons and spine surgeons in North India.

Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery

TLIF and OLIF ( fusion procedures), decompression and foraminotomy, percutaneous screws for deformity correction, intradural procedures for tumour and tethered cord through tubes, minimally invasive fixation for fracture spine and vertebroplasty.

Injection Procedures

Nerve root blocks, caudal epidural, discogram, facet injection and ganglion block

Tarun Garg

Dr. Tarun Garg is internationally trained neurosurgeon with 13 years of experience. He done his MBBS from Prestigious Government Medical College, Patiala, Then he pursue his Master of Surgery ( MS) From Government Medical College and Rajendra Hospital Patiala. He pursued his Mch ( Masters of Chirurgery in Neurosurgery from Dayanand Medical college and Hospital Ludhiana. After Mch he worked as Registrar AIMSR in Bathinda. He has keen interest in Brain Tumors, Spine Diseases ( Disc. Prolapse), Listhesis, Spine Fractures, Spine TB Etc, Endoscopic Brain and Spine Surgery, Aneurysm etc.
We have modular OTs, highly skilled Neurosurgery team and trained staff we aim to provide the best quality to the patients.
